Is this safe
for your pet?

Type any food. Pick your pet. Get a clear answer — with portion guidance and what to watch for.

Because "can my dog eat this?" shouldn't require a vet visit

PetPlate gives you fast, clear answers about what's safe to feed your pet — and what to keep far away from them. We cover dogs, cats, rabbits, and birds, with portion guidance based on your pet's weight.

Some of the most common pet toxicity emergencies come from foods that seem totally harmless — grapes, xylitol, onions, avocado. PetPlate helps you check before you share a snack.

Always check with your vet for medical concerns. PetPlate is for general guidance only. Part of PlateHub.
