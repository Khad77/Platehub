Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

PlateHub does not collect, store, or sell any personal information. There is no account system, no login, and no database of user data.

How the AI tools work

LazyPlate, PetPlate, and SteadyPlate use the Anthropic Claude API to generate responses. When you use these tools, your input (ingredients, food names, pet type) is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. This data is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

We do not log, store, or have access to the queries you send or the responses you receive.

Cookies and analytics

PlateHub may use Google Analytics and Google AdSense, which set cookies to measure site traffic and serve relevant ads. You can opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Local storage

SteadyPlate stores your meal plan data in your browser's local storage so your data persists between sessions. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

Third-party advertising

PlateHub displays ads through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Children

PlateHub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? You can reach us through the contact form on our site.
